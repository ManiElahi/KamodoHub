Our Mission

Our commitment at KomodoHub involves bringing digital advancement to Indonesian animal conservation. The platform brings together communities and schools with conservation enthusiasts to protect disappearing natural species while building environmental knowledge.

Who We Are?

Yayasan Komodo functions as the driver behind KomodoHub under its mission to defend Indonesia's diverse biodiversity. With substantial financial backing and organizational help, we created a digital framework that empowers people and institutions to study, support, and actively engage in species preservation.

What We Do?

Educate & Engage – We offer an extensive knowledge base on Indonesian wildlife and conservation programs tailored for students, teachers, and communities.

Support Conservation Efforts – Our platform enables users to contribute by reporting wildlife sightings, taking part in conservation initiatives, and sharing research.

Empower Communities – Local communities and schools are integral to preserving biodiversity, and we provide them with the necessary tools and resources.

Ensure Digital Security – Protecting user data, especially that of children, is paramount. KomodoHub adheres to strict security and privacy standards to ensure a safe and secure experience.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Conservation is a collective effort. Whether you're an educator, student, researcher, or wildlife enthusiast, you can be a part of KomodoHub and help safeguard Indonesia's precious wildlife for future generations.

Let’s make a change—one digital step at a time.
